About Sambhog

Empowering individuals and couples with knowledge for healthier, more fulfilling relationships and intimate lives.

Our Mission

Sambhog is dedicated to destigmatizing conversations around sexual wellness by providing accurate, accessible, and culturally-sensitive information. We believe everyone deserves knowledge that empowers them to make informed choices about their intimate lives.

What We Do

Sambhog is a sexual wellness and health app designed to disseminate expert-backed information in bite-sized learnings. We make complex topics around sexual health, intimacy, and relationships easy to understand and apply in daily life.

Our content is carefully curated by experts to ensure you receive reliable information that promotes holistic sexual wellbeing and healthier relationships.

Our Approach

We believe that sexual wellness is a crucial component of overall health that deserves open, shame-free discussion. Through our bite-sized educational content, we aim to:


  • Make sexual health education accessible to everyone
  • Provide evidence-based information in an easy-to-understand format
  • Foster healthier attitudes toward sexuality and intimacy
  • Support individuals in building fulfilling relationships
