Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2025

Contact Email: chitramapp@gmail.com

Sambhog app offers both modes of payment: one-time payments and subscriptions. This policy outlines our approach to cancellations, refunds, and subscription management.

Payment Types

One-Time Payments

Payments once made cannot be canceled on the platform; however, we do provide a 7-day money-back guarantee in case a customer is not satisfied with the offering.

To request a cancellation within 7 days, please contact us at chitramapp@gmail.com along with your phone number used for signing up on the platform.

Subscriptions

For subscriptions, you can cancel them directly through the platform where the payment was made. Typically, the option to cancel can be found under the "Payments and Subscriptions" tab of the respective payment method.

If you cancel your subscription, it will stop for subsequent billing cycles, but the current billing cycle will remain active.

Refund Process

Once you contact our customer care team, they will revert within 7 business days. Our customer care representatives will connect with you to understand the issue and, at their discretion, process a refund.

Once approved, the refunded amount will be credited to the respective payment method within 7 business days.

Important Notes:

  • Payments made using referral coupons are not eligible for refunds, irrespective of the time elapsed since the payment.
  • Refunds may take up to 7 business days to reflect in your account due to processing delays by the bank.
  • If the refunded amount is not received after 7 days, please contact us at chitramapp@gmail.com with your registered phone number.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund under our 7-day money-back guarantee, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to chitramapp@gmail.com
  2. Include your registered phone number in the email
  3. Briefly explain your reason for requesting a refund
  4. Provide any relevant transaction details (date of purchase, amount)

Our team will review your request and respond within 7 business days.
