Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't I see the exact pricing on the website?

We offer different pricing tiers and promotional offers that are regularly updated within our app. To ensure you always see the most current pricing and any available special offers, we keep our detailed pricing information within the app.

What payment types do you accept?

We accept a wide range of payment methods through our secure payment processor, Razorpay, including credit and debit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular digital wallets.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the content. To request a refund, contact us at chitramapp@gmail.com with your registered phone number. Note that payments made using referral coupons are not eligible for refunds.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription directly through your payment platform (Google Play). Navigate to "Payments and Subscriptions" in your account settings to manage your subscription. If you cancel, you'll still have access until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer any discount codes?

Yes, we occasionally offer promotional discounts and referral codes. These will be visible within the app upon download, so you can take advantage of any current special offers.
