At Sambhog, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines what data we access, how we use it, and your rights as a user of our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data such as your name, phone number, or email. Our app is designed to offer a seamless, login-free experience.

However, for functionality and security, we may access limited technical data:

a) Device Information

We use the permission android.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE solely to:

  • Uniquely identify your device for managing subscription access
  • Tie unlocked video content to your device without requiring sign-up or OTP
  • Prevent abuse and ensure fair access across devices

This information is used locally and is not stored, shared, or linked to your identity.

b) Payment Information

Payments for unlocking video content are securely processed via Razorpay. We do not collect or store any credit/debit card, UPI, or bank details. Razorpay's privacy policy is available at: https://razorpay.com/privacy

c) App Performance & Crash Logs

Anonymous data may be collected (if enabled) to detect bugs or improve performance. This does not include any personal or device-identifying information.

2. How We Use This Information

We use device and technical data to:

  • Manage and verify access to purchased video content
  • Maintain a smooth, login-free experience
  • Improve app performance through technical diagnostics

We do not sell or trade your data to any third parties.

3. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect or store data from children under 18. If you believe a minor has accessed the app inappropriately, please contact us.

4. Your Rights

As we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data stored to modify or delete. For any concerns or clarifications, you may contact us at:
